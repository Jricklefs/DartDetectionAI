How to Focus Your Camera

  1. Point your camera at the dartboard
  2. Click "Start Focus Check" to begin live monitoring
  3. Slowly rotate the focus ring on your camera lens
  4. Watch the score - stop when it peaks (aim for 70+)
  5. The dartboard wires should look sharp, not blurry
  6. Once focused, proceed to calibration
